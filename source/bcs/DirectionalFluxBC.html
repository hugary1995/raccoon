DirectionalFluxBC

Applies a directional flux multiplied by the surface normal vector. Can utilize the self shadowing calculation from a SelfShadowSideUserObject.

The flux is only applied on the irradiation-facing side of the surface, i.e when the product of flux vector and (outward facing) normal is negative.

A SelfShadowSideUserObject can be supplied to take self shadowing into account.

[BCs<<<{"href": "../../syntax/BCs/index.html"}>>>]
  [flux_v]
    type = DirectionalFluxBC<<<{"description": "Applies a directional flux multiplied by the surface normal vector. Can utilize the self shadowing calculation from a SelfShadowSideUserObject.", "href": "DirectionalFluxBC.html"}>>>
    variable<<<{"description": "The name of the variable that this residual object operates on"}>>> = v
    boundary<<<{"description": "The list of boundary IDs from the mesh where this object applies"}>>> = outer
    self_shadow_uo<<<{"description": "SelfShadowSideUserObject that calculates the illumination state of a side set"}>>> = shadow
  []
