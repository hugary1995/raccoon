  • temperature_radiationThe radiative temperature. A functor is any of the following: a variable, a functor material property, a function, a postprocessor or a number.

    C++ Type:MooseFunctorName

    Controllable:No

    Description:The radiative temperature. A functor is any of the following: a variable, a functor material property, a function, a postprocessor or a number.

    Unit:(no unit assumed)

    Controllable:No

    Description:The name of the variable whose linear system this object contributes to
