(AD)ConvectionHeatFluxFunctorMaterial

This functor material computes a convection heat flux between a solid surface and a fluid using functors:

where

  • is the heat transfer coefficient, given by "htc",

  • is the outward normal unit vector from the solid surface,

  • is the solid temperature, given by "T_solid", and

  • is the fluid temperature, given by "T_fluid".

The AD version of this class is used to retrieve all of the input functors with AD types.

  • T_fluidFluid temperature functor. A functor is any of the following: a variable, a functor material property, a function, a post-processor, or a number.

    Description:Fluid temperature functor. A functor is any of the following: a variable, a functor material property, a function, a post-processor, or a number.

  • T_solidSolid temperature functor. A functor is any of the following: a variable, a functor material property, a function, a post-processor, or a number.

    C++ Type:MooseFunctorName

    Controllable:No
