SLKKSPhaseConcentration

Sublattice KKS model kernel to enforce the decomposition of concentration into phase and sublattice concentrations The non-linear variable of this kernel is a sublattice concentration of phase b.

This class is the two-phase version of SLKKSMultiPhaseConcentration.

For a sub lattice KKS (SLKKS) model with two phases and , the phase concentration constraint equation is where indexes a component and a sublattice in the given phase. and is the fraction of sublattice sites in phases and . Thus is the global concentration of component and is the phase switching function.
