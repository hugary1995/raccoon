StressBasedChemicalPotential

Chemical potential from stress

The chemical potential is computed from the previous time step value of the stress tensor , the direction tensor and a user-specified prefactor Real-value material property .

The derivative of the chemical potential with regards to a concentration variable is only computed if the "c" parameter for the concentration variable is passed, and only captures the dependency of on .

warningwarning

Because the previous time step value of the stress is used, this coupling is naturally explicit and first order in time with regards to the stress.
