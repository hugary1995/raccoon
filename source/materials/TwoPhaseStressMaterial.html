TwoPhaseStressMaterial

Compute a global stress in a two phase model

The TwoPhaseStressMaterial is used in coupled phase field - solid mechanics calculations to compute the mechanical deformation of two phase regions.

The switching (mixing) coefficient which blends the two phase stresses together must be provided as a material property, using the "h" parameter.

The material also defines the (rank four tensor of) derivatives of the stress with regards to the strain, accounting for both phases' derivatives but neglecting any dependence of the extra global stress.
