MathEBFreeEnergy

Material that implements the math free energy using the expression builder and automatic differentiation

Implements the bulk free energy density

using the ExpressionBuilder system, where (c) is a coupled concentration. The minima of this free energy density lie at and . See also SplitCHMath.

Input Parameters

  • cConcentration variable

    C++ Type:std::vector<VariableName>

    Unit:(no unit assumed)

    Controllable:No

    Description:Concentration variable
