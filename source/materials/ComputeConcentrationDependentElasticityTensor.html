ComputeConcentrationDependentElasticityTensor

Compute concentration dependent elasticity tensor.

The concentration variable , specified using the "c", acts as a mixing function for the two elasticity tensors, and , specified in the "C0_ijkl" and "C1_ijkl" vector parameters. See Compute Elasticity Tensor for an explanation about the fill methods, e.g. how to input a tensor as a vector parameter.
