RegularSolutionFreeEnergy

Material that implements the free energy of a regular solution

This material defines the Helmholtz free energy density of a binary regular solution.

where ("omega") is the regular solution parameter.

    C++ Type:std::vector<VariableName>

    Controllable:No

    Description:Concentration variable
