ADCHSplitConcentration

Concentration kernel in Split Cahn-Hilliard that solves chemical potential in a weak form

Uses automatic differentiation to solve the Cahn-Hilliard equation using chemical potential as non-linear variable. Typically used with ADCHSplitChemicalPotential and can combined with ADCHSoretMobility to incorporate the effects of thermodiffusion.
