MixedSwitchingFunctionMaterial

Helper material to provide h(eta) and its derivative in one of two polynomial forms. MIX234 and MIX246

Order MIX234

Computes a switching function represented by 234-order polynomial, which is the general form with an adjustable parameter ().

Order MIX246

Computes a switching function represented by 246-order polynomial, which is the general form with an adjustable parameter ().
